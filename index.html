About Wei Da

Wei Da is a dedicated climber specializing in bouldering, with a hardest outdoor boulder grade of V12. An expert in Moonboard training, he offers personalized coaching sessions to help climbers achieve new heights. With a sharp eye for micro-beta, Wei Da provides climbers with tailored insights and techniques to push their performance and reach their climbing goals.
